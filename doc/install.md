## Requirements

* OpenOffice or LibreOffice and its SDK, version 3.4 or newer
* Ruby 1.9.X or newer


## Building Gem

    > source ~/openoffice.org3.4_sdk/host_name/setsdkenv_unix.sh
    > rake gem

Resulting gem is generated under pkg/.
